# Hotel Booking Data Analysis

This project performs an end-to-end Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on **119k+ hotel booking records** to identify cancellation patterns, customer behavior, and key business insights. The analysis was carried out using **Excel PivotTables, Conditional Formatting, and Excel-based visual exploration**.

---

## üìå Project Overview
The goal of this analysis is to understand:
- Why customers cancel hotel bookings  
- Which hotel types have higher cancellation rates  
- How seasonality and demand affect booking patterns  
- What actionable strategies can reduce cancellations and improve customer retention  

This project is fully Excel-driven and demonstrates the ability to clean, analyze, visualize, and interpret large datasets using spreadsheet-based analytics.

---

## üìä Key Findings

### **1. City hotels have the highest cancellation rates**
City hotels showed significantly higher cancellations compared to resort hotels, especially during **peak-demand months**.

### **2. Seasonal patterns impact cancellations**
Specific months displayed spikes in cancellation volume, indicating demand sensitivity and pricing influence.

### **3. Customer behavior trends**
Analysis of repeated guests, market segments, and lead times revealed clear patterns affecting confirmation and cancellation decisions.

## üìå Recommendations
Based on EDA insights:

1. **Dynamic pricing** during peak cancellation periods  
2. **Stricter confirmation/advance payment rules** for city hotels  
3. **Customer retention programs** for frequently canceling segments  
4. **Improved booking reminders** for high lead-time bookings
